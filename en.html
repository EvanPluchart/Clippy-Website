Frequently asked questions

The details that matter.

Still have a question? Open an issue on GitHub.

Is Clippy free? +

Yes. Clippy is free and released under the MIT License. Anyone can inspect, audit, and improve its source code.

Why does Clippy ask for Accessibility permission? +

It lets Clippy send the paste shortcut to the app you were using after you select an item. Clipboard monitoring and search remain local.

Which macOS versions are supported? +

Clippy requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later. It is designed for both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

Does Clippy send my clipboard over the Internet? +

No. Your history is stored on your Mac. This website only checks GitHub when it loads to offer the latest available release.
